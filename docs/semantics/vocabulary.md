# Vocabulary

- flow: a named specification of steps and their dependencies.
- step: the minimal unit of execution within a flow.
- execution: a single run of a flow against declared inputs.
- artifact: an immutable record produced by a step or derivation.
- evidence: a record used to support verification decisions.
- verification: the determination of whether requirements are met.
- replay: re-running a flow under declared acceptability constraints.
- dataset: the versioned data corpus bound to a flow run.
- replay envelope: the statistical acceptability bounds used for replay validation.
